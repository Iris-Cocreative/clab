Alumni stories

What people say, months after the retreat.

These are drafts, pulled from long conversations.

A few are video interviews, still being edited. A few are written by alumni themselves, slowly, in the time they had. None of them are polished marketing and none of them are trying to sound like a testimonial.

We publish them when they feel true enough to the person who lived them. Sometimes that takes a year.

What's coming up

The next few gatherings.

A monthly circle, a couple of retreats a year, seasonal things as they come.

That's the rhythm. We don't pack the calendar. The next three dates are below. If you're on the letters list, you'll hear about the rest first.

April 26

Sunday Circle — online, 90 minutes

Our monthly online circle. A short practice, some quiet, a question we pass around. Newcomers welcome. Bring tea.

May 18

Alumni Reunion — online

For anyone who's been through a retreat. A long sit, a slower check-in, and a chance to hear how the work has kept working on people. Closed group.

June 8–14

Costa Rica Retreat — Solstice

Six days on the Pacific coast. Group of ten. By application. A few scholarship seats are open; write us if cost is the thing keeping you out.
