The Work

Maps, teachings, and doorways.

The work of C.Lab bridges philosophy and participation — a living curriculum of frameworks, retreats, gatherings, and one-on-one passages. Each is a doorway into the same underlying field.

The Framework

Five Movements of Transformation.

The 5 Movements are the spine of the work — a living arc that structures our retreats and the ongoing practice beyond them.

They are not stages to complete, but currents to move with. Each movement opens the next, and all of them return to one another as the journey deepens.

I

Awakening

Sensing that a deeper life is possible.

II

Conditioning

Seeing the box we've been living in.

III

Reconnection

Coming home to body, heart, and earth.

IV

Contribution

Turning what we've found toward service.

V

Communion

Living from, and for, the whole.

Retreats

Immersive passages.

Multi-day retreats are the primary doorway into the work — not peak experiences, but real shifts in how we live, love, and lead.

Each retreat is held in an intimate group of ten and moves through the 5 Movements of Transformation. The container is built on safety, depth, and relational practice.

We have been invitation-only for years. Opening this door is new — and the ethos of personal connection remains. Every application is the beginning of a conversation.

Zoom Gatherings

Open gatherings, every month.

A low-barrier doorway into the living field — facilitator-led, open to newcomers and alumni alike.

Each month we gather online to practice together, share from where we are, and deepen into the work. No application required. Come as you are, and bring a friend.

These gatherings are often the first taste of C.Lab — a way to sense the field before stepping into a retreat.
