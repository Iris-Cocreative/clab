What We Are

We run retreats. We hold gatherings. We're beginning to train the people who'll carry this work home.

That's the outer form.

Inside it, something older is going on. The people who find us are usually tired. Tired of performing a life. Tired of the optimizations and the ten-step frameworks. Tired of being told what they should want.

They're ready for something slower. More honest. More rooted.

We can't promise transformation. What we can offer is a container that has held up across six retreats and four continents, and a group of people doing real work alongside you in it.

Four words we use a lot: consciousness, community, creativity, contribution. They describe what tends to grow when people sit with each other this way.

An Invitation

If the promises of consumerism have stopped working on you.

If you've felt the quiet suspicion that a more real way of being alive is possible.

If you're done trying to escape the world, and ready to wake up inside it:

you're in the right place.

We aren't going to optimize you. We're going to sit with you while you remember something you already know.
