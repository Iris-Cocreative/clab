Support the work

Where your gift goes.

C.Lab is a registered nonprofit. Donations fund scholarship seats, the monthly Zoom infrastructure, facilitator training stipends, and Jenny and Forest's time, which has been underpaid for a long time.

The short version: donations are what let us keep the retreats small, the entry fee low, and nobody turned away for money alone.

Commitment to accessibility and scholarship support is part of how we do this. It's for the children of our children, and all of life. We'd rather stay small and keep it honest than scale and lose the thread.

Scholarships

If money is the thing, tell us.

We keep a scholarship fund. It comes from donations and from alumni paying forward. If you want to come and can't pay full fare, write to us. We don't promise anything, but no one has been turned away for money alone, and we'd like to keep it that way.
